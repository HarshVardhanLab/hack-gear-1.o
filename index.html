FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS THE SIZE OF TEAM?

Teams can have 2-4 members. Solo participants are allowed but team collaboration is encouraged.

IS THERE ANY PARTICIPATION FEES?

No! Participation is completely free of charge. Just bring your skills and enthusiasm!

WHAT ARE THE ENTRY TIMINGS TO THE COLLEGE DURING THE HACKATHON?

Entry to the college campus will be allowed from 7:00 AM onwards on April 30. After initial check-in and verification.

HACKATHON IS ONLINE OR OFFLINE?

Hybrid format! Ideation phase online, building phase offline at our campus venue.

FOOD & LIVING FACILITY?

Food provisions and basic amenities will be provided throughout the offline phase.

WILL TRANSPORT FACILITIES BE PROVIDED FOR PARTICIPANTS DURING THE HACKATHON?

No, transport facilities will not be provided by the organizers. Participants are requested to arrange their own travel to and from the venue.
