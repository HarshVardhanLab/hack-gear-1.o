why hack gear 1.0?

Hack Gear 1.0 isn’t just a hackathon —
It’s Aligarh’s first tech battleground, where developers, designers, and problem-solvers come together to turn groundbreaking ideas into real-world tech solutions. From AI to Blockchain, from cybersecurity to web development — this is where innovation meets execution. Whether you're a seasoned coder or a first-time hacker, Hack Gear 1.0 gives you the platform to collaborate, create, and compete with the best minds in the region.

🔹Why Join?

  • 💡 Challenge Yourself – Tackle real-world problem statements
  • ⚙ Hybrid Format – Ideate online, build offline
  • 🧠 Mentorship Access – Learn from tech leaders & industry experts
  • 🏆 Recognition & Rewards – Prizes, swags, internship opportunities
  • 🌐 Tech Themes – AI, Web Dev, Blockchain, Cybersecurity & more

what will you get?

recognition

🤝 Connect with fellow innovators, mentors, and industry experts throughout the event. 💬 Get a chance to collaborate with like-minded tech enthusiasts from different colleges. 🎙 Interact with judges, speakers, and sponsors during the sessions and breaks. 🌐 Build your professional network for future internships, jobs, and collaborations. 📲 Stay in touch post-event through community groups and online platforms.

certification

📜 Every participant will receive a Certificate of Participation from Vision Institute of Technology, Aligarh. 🏆 Winners and runner-ups will be awarded Certificates of Excellence along with prizes. 🔖 Theme-wise best projects will get Special Recognition Certificates to highlight their innovation. 💼 These certificates can be a great addition to your portfolio and boost your resume for future opportunities.

networking

🏅 All participants will receive official certificates of recognition from Vision Institute of Technology, Aligarh. 🥇 Top teams will be honored with certificates of excellence and exclusive accolades in their respective themes. 🚀 Outstanding innovations may be highlighted in post-event blogs, college showcases, and partner platforms. 📢 Teams will get a chance to present live in front of judges and industry mentors during the finale.
